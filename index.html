Podcast CPM calculator






What is CPM?

CPM stands for ‘cost per mille’. It is a standard practice across virtually all digital marketing. The basic concept behind CPM is that the advertising company can pay per 1,000 views/listens/clicks.

With a CPM rate, advertisers will know what they need to pay in order to reach a 1000 listeners.

How to set your CPM?

The CPM formula is: CPM = 1000 x price / average number of listeners.

And generally most podcasters will set their CPM-rate between $15-40.

As an example. If you have 1500 listeners per episode and you set your price for a host-read ad at $25, your CPM will be $16. ($25 x 1000 / 1500 = $16)

CPM is a very straightforward concept. Once you have set your CPM rate, you will look much more professional when communicating with advertisers.
